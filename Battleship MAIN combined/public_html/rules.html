Battle Station

This is what each player's Battle Station looks like, here's where all the action takes place!

Ship Selector

You may select the ships you want to set, and scroll by clicking on the arrow icon.

Ship Placement

You may set your ship by clicking on the beginning of cell, followed by clicking the end cell after.

Ship Placement

You can set ships both vertically and horizontally by clicking on the cells, and setting them in your preferred direction

Setting your board

Once all ships are set, you can set your board and begin the battle against your enemy!

Miss

The "Miss" icon is displayed if your attack has missed the enemy's ship

Hit

The "Hit" icon is displayed if your attack has hit the enemy's ship
